Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you redeem a code, we collect your name and email (if provided). Your shipping address is sent directly to Hermes (on Hack Club infrastructure) and is not stored here.

How we use it

Your name and email are stored only to track which codes have been redeemed. Your address is forwarded to Hermes to ship your package and is not retained by CODES.

Third parties

Your shipping information is shared with Hermes (Hack Club's fulfillment service) solely for delivery purposes. We do not sell or share your data with anyone else.

Open source

This project is fully open source. You can review the code at github.com/jeninh/codes.

Contact

Questions? Reach out to jenin@hackclub.com.
